Chapter 4 — Intermediate Techniques

4.1 Barre Chords

Barre chords use one finger to press multiple strings, creating movable chord shapes. The two most common shapes are the E-shape and A-shape.

E-shape barre (root on 6th string) 
Bass  E   A   D   G   B   e
Fret  1   2   3   3   2   1
e|--1---1---1---1---1---1---|
B|--1---1---1---1---1---1---|
G|--1---1---1---1---1---1---|
D|--3---3---3---3---3---3---|
A|--3---3---3---3---3---3---|
E|--1---1---1---1---1---1---|  (barre at fret 1, shape like open E)
A-shape barre (root on 5th string) 
Bass  E   A   D   G   B   e
Fret  x   1   3   3   3   1
e|--1---1---1---1---1---1---|
B|--3---3---3---3---3---3---|
G|--3---3---3---3---3---3---|
D|--3---3---3---3---3---3---|
A|--1---1---1---1---1---1---|
E|--x---x---x---x---x---x---|  (barre at fret 1, shape like open A)
Barre tips
  • Roll your index finger slightly toward its side for strength.
  • Keep the wrist straight and elbow under the neck.
  • Practice partial barre (2–3 strings) before full barre.

4.3 Intermediate Fingerstyle

Combine thumb bass with pattern-based picking to create flowing arrangements.

// Travis picking pattern (Em key)
  p   i   p   m   p   i   p   a
e|------0-------0-----|
B|----0---0---0---0---|
G|--0-------0-------0-|
D|--------------------|
A|2-------2-------2---|
E|0-------0-------0---|
Variation

Accentuate bass notes (p) on beats 1 & 3, melody fingers (i/m/a) on off-beats.

4.4 Arpeggio & Accompaniment

Create movement within chords by picking individual notes in sequence.

// C major arpeggio
e|------0-------0-----|
B|----1---1---1---1---|
G|--0-------0-------0-|
D|--------------------|
A|3-------3-------3---|
E|--------------------|
Accompaniment idea

Alternate between block strum and arpeggio every other bar for contrast.

4.5 Dynamics & Accents

Control loudness and accents to shape your performance.

Soft vs loud
  • Light pick pressure for quiet passages.
  • Stronger attack for emphasis.
Accents (>)

Mark accented beats to stand out in a pattern.

// Pattern with accent on 2 & 4
| D   U   D   U |
| 1 > 2 3 > 4 &|
