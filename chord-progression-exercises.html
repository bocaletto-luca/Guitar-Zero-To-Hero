1. I–IV–V in C, G, D

Classic progression: play four bars of each chord, 4 cycles at ♩=80.

// In C major (C–F–G)
| C | F | G | G |
e|--0---1---3---3---|
B|--1---1---3---3---|
G|--0---2---0---0---|
D|--2---3---0---0---|
A|--3---3---2---2---|
E|--x---x---3---3---|

// In G major (G–C–D)
| G | C | D | D |
e|--3---0---2---2---|
B|--3---1---3---3---|
G|--0---0---2---2---|
D|--0---2---0---0---|
A|--2---3---x---x---|
E|--3---x---x---x---|

3. Minor Cadence Drill

Practice i–VII–VI–VII in A minor, building tension resolving back to i.

// A minor cadence
| Am | G  | F  | G  |
e|--0----3----1----3----|
B|--1----3----1----3----|
G|--2----0----2----0----|
D|--2----0----3----0----|
A|--0----2----3----2----|
E|--x----3----1----3----|  ×6 cycles at ♩=75

4. Tempo Variation Practice

Play I–IV–V in C at ♩=60, 80, 100, then back down. Develop control across speeds.

// 4 bars each chord at multiple BPM
| C | F | G | G |  at ♩=60
| C | F | G | G |  at ♩=80
| C | F | G | G |  at ♩=100
| C | F | G | G |  at ♩=80
| C | F | G | G |  at ♩=60

5. Modulation & Key Change

Shift the I–IV–V sequence up a whole step: C → D. Practice smooth transition.

// C major 2 bars, then shift to D major 2 bars
| C | F |  →  | D | G |
e|--0---1--------2---3--|
B|--1---1--------3---3--|
G|--0---2--------2---0--|
D|--2---3--------0---0--|
A|--3---3--------x---2--|
E|--x---1--------x---3--|  ×4 cycles at ♩=70
