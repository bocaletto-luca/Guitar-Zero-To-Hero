1. Introduction

Folk guitar sits at the heart of storytelling and song. Emphasizing open chords, simple accompaniments and accessible fingerwork, it invites players to accompany vocals or narrate melodies.

2. Open Chord Progressions

Common four-chord progressions underpin countless folk songs.

5. Alternate Tunings

Unlock droning possibilities and new chord voicings.

// Drop D: D A D G B e
D–A–D–G–B–e shapes unchanged except low string

// DADGAD: D A D G A D
  DADGAD allows modal, open fifths and simple fingerstyle

6. Capo Techniques

Use capo to change key while preserving open shapes.

// Key of A with capo 2 (capo on fret 2):
Shapes: G – D – Em – C
Sounds: A – E – F#m – D
