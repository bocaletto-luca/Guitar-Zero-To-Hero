4. Strumming Patterns

Combine chord shapes with varied rhythms at ♪=80.

// Pattern A: downstrums
| D   D   D   D |

// Pattern B: eighths
| D U D U D U D U |

// Pattern C: down-down-up-up-down-up
| D D U U D U |

5. Transition Drills

Switch smoothly between shapes; start slow at ♩=60.

// C → G → Am → F
| C |  G |  Am |  F |
e|--0---3---0---1---|
B|--1---3---1---1---|
G|--0---0---2---2---|
D|--2---0---2---3---|
A|--3---2---0---3---|
E|--x---3---x---1---|  ×4 cycles

// Em → D → C → G
| Em |  D |  C |  G |
e|--0---2---0---3---|
B|--0---3---1---3---|
G|--0---2---0---0---|
D|--2---0---2---0---|
A|--2---x---3---2---|
E|--0---x---x---3---|  ×4 cycles
