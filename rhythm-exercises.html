Lock Your Timing & Groove

Subdivisions, syncopation, ghost notes and fluid strum patterns in your daily routine.

1. Subdivisions & Counting

Internalize quarter, eighth and sixteenth subdivisions. Clap or tap before playing.

// Quarter = 4 beats
1   2   3   4

// Eighth = 8 taps
1-&-2-&-3-&-4-&

// Sixteenth = 16 taps
1-e-&-a-2-e-&-a-3-e-&-a-4-e-&-a

2. Downstroke & Upstroke Patterns

Develop a smooth D/U motion with consistent timing at ♩=60 → 90.

// Basic eighths
| D U D U D U D U |  
  1-&-2-&-3-&-4-&  

// Accent pattern
| D U D U | D U D U |
| 1-(&)-2-(–)-3-(&)-4-(–)|

3. Syncopation & Ghost Notes

Add off-beat accents and percussive “x” hits.

// Syncopated strum (eighths + rests)
| D - U D - U - D |  
| 1   & 2   &   4 |

// Ghost note pattern
e|--x---0---x---0---|
B|--x---0---x---0---|
G|--x---0---x---0---|
D|--x---0---x---0---|
A|--x---0---x---0---|
E|--x---0---x---0---|

4. Chord-Strum Blends

Combine chord shapes with rhythmic variation.

// G chord, eighth pattern
  D   U   D   U
e|--3-------3-------|
B|--0---0---0---0---|
G|--0-------0-------|
D|--0---0---0---0---|
A|--2-------2-------|
E|--3---3---3---3---|

// Em chord with ghost & accent
  >   x   >   x
e|--0---x---0---x---|
B|--0---x---0---x---|
G|--0---x---0---x---|
D|--2---x---2---x---|
A|--2---x---2---x---|
E|--0---x---0---x---|

5. Practice Plan

  • Subdivisions clapping – 3 min
  • Down/up patterns – 5 min
  • Syncopation & ghost notes – 5 min
  • Chord-strum blends – 7 min

1. Finger Assignment

p = thumb (E/A/D), i = index (G), m = middle (B), a = ring (e). Keep fingers curved, hand relaxed.

// Assignment grid
  p   i   m   a
E|0   -   -   -|
A|-   0   -   -|
D|-   -   0   -|
G|-   -   -   0|
B|-   -   -   0|
e|-   -   -   0|

2. Basic Finger Rolls

Roll across strings with p-i-m-a pattern.

// C chord roll at ♩=60
  p   i   m   a
e|------0-------|
B|----1---1-----|
G|--0-------0---|
D|2-----------2-|
A|3-------------|
E|--------------|

3. Travis Picking Variations

Steady alternating bass (p) with melody fingers.

// Em Travis at ♪=80
  p   i   p   m   p   i   p   a
e|------0-------0-------0-------0---|
B|----0---0---0---0---0---0---0---0-|
G|--0-------0-------0-------0-------|
D|-----------------------------------|
A|2-------2-------2-------2---------|
E|0-------0-------0-------0---------|

4. Alternating Bass Patterns

Thumb alternates between root and fifth.

// C chord alt-bass at ♩=70
  p       p   i m
e|--------0-------|
B|------1---1-----|
G|----0-------0---|
D|----------------|
A|3-------3-------|
E|----------------|

5. Practice Routine

  • Finger rolls – 5 min
  • Travis picking – 7 min
  • Alternating bass – 5 min
  • Free fingerstyle – 3 min
